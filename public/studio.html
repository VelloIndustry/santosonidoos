🏆 Latin Grammy Nominated · 2025 · Engineering

Medellín is where Latin music is being made right now. Santo Sonido is inside El Poblado — the neighborhood where producers, engineers, and artists operate. Fly in. Record. Leave with a finished record and a video.

American and European artists are discovering what Colombian musicians have known for years: Medellín has world-class infrastructure, a fraction of the cost of LA or London, and a culture that feeds the music. This is a destination with a purpose — not just a city to visit.

Our team is English-speaking. You don't need to navigate a language barrier. You just need to show up with ideas.

✈️

Direct Flights

Non-stop from Miami (3h), New York (5.5h), and Madrid (10h). Copa, Avianca, and American all fly direct. Easy connection from any major hub.

🛂

Visa-Free Entry

US and EU passport holders enter Colombia visa-free for up to 90 days. No paperwork, no waiting. Land at José María Córdova and head straight to the studio.

💰

Real Cost Advantage

Studio rates, Airbnb, food, transport — significantly cheaper than LA, Miami, or London. The same budget goes 3–4x further. More studio time. More creative room.

🏙️

El Poblado Neighborhood

Walkable, safe, international. Dozens of Airbnbs within a 5-minute walk of the studio. Restaurants, bars, and nightlife that never stop. The neighborhood feeds the sessions.

🎵

Latin Urban Capital

Reggaeton, Latin trap, afrobeats fusion — Medellín is where these sounds are evolving. The city's cultural energy transfers directly into the room if you let it.

🌐

English-Speaking Team

Fully bilingual engineering team. No translation friction, no communication gaps. We speak the same language about music — and the literal one too.

What's Included

Everything
the session
needs.

Every rental includes access to the full signal chain, DAW workstation, and studio monitors. Add engineering, camera, or post-production services to any session block.

🎙

Recording Booth

Acoustically treated vocal booth with professional condenser and dynamic mic selection. Isolation rated for voice, acoustic instruments, and loud sources.

🎛

Control Room

Full control room with calibrated monitoring, hardware outboard, and sight lines into the live room. Built for engineers who actually mix.

🎚

Mixing Console

Professional-grade console with analog summing. Direct integration with digital workstations — hybrid workflow, fully supported.

🔇

Isolation Rooms

Multiple isolation spaces for tracking drums, live instruments, or simultaneous multi-performer sessions. Full acoustic separation available.

🖥

DAW Workstation

Industry-standard DAWs pre-loaded: Pro Tools, Ableton Live, Logic Pro. Plugin library available. Bring your own session or work from scratch.

🎬

Video Production

Full camera setup available for music videos, visualizers, and social content. Shot and edited in-house. Add to any studio rental.

📷

Photography

In-studio photo sessions for press shots, album artwork, and promotional content. LATAM-ready assets, same-day turnaround available.

🔊

Reference Monitoring

Calibrated studio monitors + headphone rigs across three listening environments. What sounds right here sounds right everywhere.
